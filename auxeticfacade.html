Influx: Letting Light Play

Influx is a dynamic surface that choreographs the balance between light and shade. Through transformable structures and iridescent materials, it playfully filters light, sometimes letting it in, sometimes holding it backâ€”creating an ever-changing interplay of color and shadow that produces vibrant, shaded space.

Radiative Cooling

This system uses simple movements and materials to modulate light softly and responsively.The project evolved as a result of the question, "How might we design a dynamic system that integrates auxetic transformation as a geometrical response to light which results in shading, combined with materials that produce visual color variation effects?"

Initial tests were carried out with foam as material. To scale the project, conventional movement technique such as hinge is done in combination with auxetic pattern. The movement takes place in 2 states, open and closed state of the mechansim.
