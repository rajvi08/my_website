Ticklish

Ticklish is a grounded haptic machine with varying intensities of movement, straddling the line between playfulness and irritation, tickle and torture. Remote communication is at play..

The machine is comprised of a mechanical crank attached to a stepper motor that allows a mounted feather to sway back and forth. An external controller attached to the machine contains a slider potentiometer that controls the intensity of the swaying.

The materials used include plywood, foam, PLA, and a faux feather. The mechanism was primarily laser cut and controller was 3D printed.
